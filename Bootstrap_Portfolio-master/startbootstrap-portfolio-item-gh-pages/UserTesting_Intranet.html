UserTesting.com Company Intranet Internal Usability Study

Project Description

The UserTesting Intranet prototype that I built was a first iteration, low fidelity series of pages designed to test the bare bones layout that our company had in mind. Prior to developing the prototype, we performed a card sorting analysis to get an understanding of how the pages should be laid out, and where the content should be placed. After the card sorting analysis, I took the data that we collected and constructed Photoshop wireframes that were then used in a cognitive walkthrough done by one of our employees. I then annotated the cognitive walkthrough for important usability and design choices, which was then applied to further iterations of the intranet.
