Demographic Study of Users Smartphone Battery Application Usage Published Empirical Study

Project Description

This demographic study was one of my projects for the Human Computer Interaction master’s program. It involved surveying eighty five participants for their smartphone application usage, as well as other demographic information such as their age, race, gender, and socioeconomic status. Smartwatch usage was also collected and analyzed. It was found from two chi square tests that in my sample of participants, women were more likely to use iOS devices compared to Android. It was also found that only one participant had a health application as his top used application, and only two participants total had any health application listed on their top ten battery usage list. The study also found that some participants would use their smartwatches for exercise related activities, but they wouldn’t use their smartphone in conjunction with their watch for exercise.

Project Details

  • Paper was published in the Journal of Software Engineering
  • Link to the journal publication: http://www.questjournals.org/jses/papers/Vol3-issue-4/A340107.pdf
